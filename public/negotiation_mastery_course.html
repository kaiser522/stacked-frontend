Complete Negotiation Process

Follow this proven negotiation framework:

1

Research & Preparation Phase

Gather property information, market data, seller motivations, and comparable sales. Set your negotiation objectives and identify potential concession points.

2

Build Rapport & Discovery

Establish connection with the other party, understand their true motivations, timeline, and constraints through strategic questioning.

3

Present Your Position

Make your initial offer with confidence, provide supporting rationale, and frame the proposal in terms of mutual benefit.

4

Handle Counteroffers & Objections

Listen actively, acknowledge concerns, reframe objections positively, and present alternative solutions that address their needs.

5

Find Creative Solutions

Explore win-win alternatives, adjust terms and conditions, consider seller financing or lease options to bridge gaps.

6

Create Urgency & Momentum

Use ethical urgency techniques, highlight market conditions, and create compelling reasons to act decisively.

7

Secure Agreement & Close

Recognize buying signals, use appropriate closing techniques, and guide the conversation toward signed contracts.

8

Follow Through & Relationship Building

Ensure smooth transaction execution, maintain positive relationships, and create opportunities for future deals and referrals.
