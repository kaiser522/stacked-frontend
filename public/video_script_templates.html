Video Recording Tips for Maximum Engagement

Hook in First 3 Seconds

Start with a bold statement, question, or visual that makes people stop scrolling. Your opening line is everything.

Show, Don't Just Tell

Use screen recordings, point to your phone, or show the actual dashboard. Visual proof builds credibility.

Speak to One Person

Look directly at the camera and speak like you're talking to a friend. Authentic conversation converts better than sales pitches.

Include Your Numbers

Replace template numbers with your actual results. "I went from X to Y deals" is more powerful than generic claims.

Clear Call-to-Action

Always end with a specific action: "Link in bio to get started" or "Comment 'LINK' for more info."

Test Different Angles

Record the same script with different emotions, settings, or visual approaches to see what resonates with your audience.

Edit the script to match your personal style and results
