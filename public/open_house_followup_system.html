Immediate Follow-Up (Same Day)
Step 1: Thank You Text/Email
Within 2 hours
Sample Text Message:
"Hi [Name], thank you for visiting the open house at [Address] today! I hope you enjoyed seeing the property. If you have any questions or would like to schedule a private showing, please let me know. Have a great rest of your weekend!"
Sample Email Subject: "Thank you for visiting [Address] today"
"Dear [Name], It was a pleasure meeting you at the open house today. I wanted to follow up and see if you have any questions about the property or the neighborhood. I've attached some additional information about the home and recent comparable sales in the area. Please don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to schedule a private tour or discuss your home buying needs."
Short-Term Follow-Up (Days 1-7)
Step 2: Personal Phone Call
Day 1-2
Phone Script Opening:
"Hi [Name], this is [Agent Name] from [Brokerage]. We met yesterday at the open house on [Address]. I wanted to personally follow up and see what you thought of the property. Do you have a few minutes to chat?"
Step 3: Value-Added Email
Day 3-4
Sample Email Content:
"Subject: Neighborhood insights for [Area Name] - Thought you'd find this interesting Hi [Name], Since you expressed interest in the [Area] neighborhood, I thought you might enjoy this information about local schools, upcoming developments, and market trends in the area. I've also included links to some similar properties that just came on the market. Feel free to reach out if any of these catch your eye!"
