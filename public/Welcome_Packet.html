How Referrals Are Tracked

Simple Link-Based Tracking

Affiliates receive a unique referral link. We use 30-day last-click attribution and links only (no public coupon field).

Example Link Format

https://get-stacked.com/partners/<handle>?ref=<affiliate_id>&utm_source=<handle>&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=<campaign>

Link Rules

  • Use your issued link - but do not post to coupon/deal sites (violates terms)
  • 30-day last-click wins attribution
  • New customers only - we block matches by email, phone, and card fingerprint/payment identifier
  • You are paid only for brand-new clients you bring
Attribution Window: 30-day last-click attribution means if a customer clicks your link, they have 30 days to make their first purchase and you'll get credit for that referral.
