Complete Your Tax Documentation

Before receiving affiliate commissions, you must complete your W-9 tax form. This ensures compliance with IRS regulations and enables smooth payout processing for all US-based affiliates.

Form W-9

Required for all affiliates

The W-9 form provides your Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) to Stacked Technologies for tax reporting purposes. You'll receive a 1099-NEC form if you earn $600+ in a calendar year.

Required Information:

Name: [Your Legal Name]
Business Name (if different): [DBA or Business Name]
Tax Classification: [Individual/LLC/Corp/etc.]
Address: [Your Current Address]
TIN (SSN/EIN): [XXX-XX-XXXX]

Important Requirements:

Legal name must match your SSN or EIN exactly as filed with the IRS. Backup withholding may apply if you don't provide a valid TIN.
