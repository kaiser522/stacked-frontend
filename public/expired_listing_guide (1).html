The Expired Listing Opportunity

Expired listings represent frustrated sellers who need help. When approached professionally with solutions, they become your best prospects.

Why Expired Listings Convert

These sellers have already demonstrated motivation to sell and understand the process. They're looking for an agent who can succeed where others failed.

Key Success Factors

Speed: Contact within 24 hours of expiration
Research: Understand why it didn't sell
Solutions: Present specific fixes, not criticism
Value: Show market knowledge and results

Phone Scripts That Work

Proven openers that get conversations started and appointments set.

Initial Phone Call Script
"Hi [Name], this is [Your name] with [Brokerage]. I noticed your home at [Address] just came off the market. I'm sure that's frustrating after being listed for [X] months. I've actually sold several homes in your area recently, and I have some ideas about what might help yours sell. Could we set up a brief meeting so I can share what I'm seeing in the market?"
Voice Mail Script
"Hi [Name], this is [Your name] from [Brokerage]. I specialize in helping homeowners who've had their homes on the market without success. I've sold [X] expired listings in the past year and I have some specific ideas for your property at [Address]. My number is [Phone] - I'd love to share what I think could make the difference. Thanks!"
Door Approach Script
"Hi [Name], I'm [Your name] with [Brokerage]. I've been working in this neighborhood for [X] years and I noticed your home was recently on the market. I actually have three buyers looking in this area right now, and I wanted to see if you're still interested in selling. I brought a quick market update to show you what's been happening locally."

Common Objections & Responses

Handle the most frequent objections with confidence and professionalism.

"We're taking a break from selling"
"I completely understand - selling can be exhausting when it doesn't go as planned. The good news is the market has shifted since you were last active. Can I show you what's changed? It might give you a different perspective, and there's no obligation."
"Our last agent said it was priced right"
"I'm sure they believed that at the time. However, the market is always changing, and what buyers are willing to pay is the only true measure of value. Let me show you what similar homes have actually sold for recently - the data might surprise you."
"We don't want to reduce the price"
"I understand not wanting to leave money on the table. Sometimes pricing isn't the issue though - it could be marketing, showing feedback, or timing. Before we talk about price, let me show you some strategies that have helped other sellers get their asking price."
"We're interviewing several agents"
"That's smart - this is an important decision. I'd appreciate the chance to show you specifically what I'll do differently to get your home sold. Most agents will promise you the same things. I'd like to show you my track record with expired listings and my detailed marketing plan."

Listing Presentation for Expired

Focus on solutions and results, not criticism of the previous agent.

Presentation Outline

Market analysis showing current activity and trends
Specific marketing plan with enhanced exposure strategy
Success stories from similar expired listings you've sold
Pricing strategy based on current market conditions
Timeline expectations and communication plan

What Not to Say

Avoid criticizing the previous agent or brokerage. Focus on what you'll do differently rather than what they did wrong. Sellers want solutions, not blame.
