What is the Calendar?

The Calendar section is your unified scheduling hub that syncs seamlessly with Apple Calendar and Google Calendar. Create, view, and manage all your appointments and events in one place, with automatic two-way synchronization ensuring your schedule is always up-to-date across all your devices.

The calendar displays events in a monthly view and provides search functionality to quickly find specific events or filter by person's name. Events created in your CRM automatically appear in your connected calendar apps, and events created in Apple Calendar or Google Calendar sync back to your CRM.

Connecting Your Calendar

Connecting Google Calendar

  1. Click the "Google" button in the calendar toolbar
  2. Sign in to your Google account when prompted
  3. Grant calendar access permissions to the CRM
  4. Select which Google calendar to sync (if you have multiple)
  5. Wait for the initial sync to complete (usually 1-2 minutes)
  6. Look for "Connected to Google Calendar" status indicator

Connecting Apple Calendar

  1. Click the "Apple" button in the calendar toolbar
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID when prompted
  3. Authorize calendar access for the CRM application
  4. Choose which Apple calendar to sync
  5. Allow the initial synchronization to finish
  6. Confirm "Connected to Apple Calendar" appears in the status bar

Troubleshooting Sync Issues

Events Not Syncing

  • Check the sync status bar‚Äîensure it shows "Connected"
  • Click the manual "Sync" button to force an immediate sync
  • Verify you're still signed in to your Google or Apple account
  • Check your internet connection
  • Wait 10-15 minutes‚Äîsome syncs take time
  • Try disconnecting and reconnecting your calendar

Events Appearing Incorrectly

  • Verify time zone settings in both the CRM and your calendar app
  • Check that dates and times were entered correctly
  • Ensure you're viewing the correct calendar in your calendar app
  • Refresh your calendar view in both applications

Duplicate Events

  • The system prevents duplicates, but manual imports can cause issues
  • Delete duplicate events from either calendar‚Äîdon't delete from both
  • After deleting duplicates, wait for sync to complete
  • Avoid manually importing events that are already synced

Connection Lost

  • Check if you've changed your Google or Apple account password
  • Click the calendar button to re-authenticate
  • Ensure you're still logged into your Google or Apple account
  • Check if calendar permissions were accidentally revoked
  • Try signing out and signing back in to reconnect

Events Not Appearing in Calendar App

  • Open your calendar app and refresh/sync manually
  • Check that you're viewing the correct synced calendar
  • Verify the event date is within the visible time range
  • Some calendar apps cache data‚Äîforce close and reopen the app
  • Check your calendar app's sync settings
Sync Troubleshooting: Most sync issues resolve themselves within 15 minutes. If problems persist beyond 30 minutes, try disconnecting and reconnecting your calendar or contact support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I connect both Google and Apple Calendar?

Yes, but you should typically connect one primary calendar. If you use both, they should already be synced with each other through your devices. Connecting both to the CRM could cause duplicate events.

What happens to my existing events when I connect?

During the initial sync, existing events from your connected calendar will be imported into the CRM. This is a one-time import and may take a few minutes depending on how many events you have.

Can I choose which events sync?

Currently, all events in the connected calendar sync automatically. If you want to keep some events private, create them in a different calendar that's not connected to the CRM.

How far back and forward does sync work?

The system syncs events from 30 days in the past to 365 days in the future. Older or far-future events are not synced to improve performance.

Will sync drain my phone battery?

No. Calendar sync happens through cloud services, not directly on your phone. Battery usage is equivalent to normal calendar app usage.

What if I don't want to sync my calendar?

Calendar sync is optional. You can use the CRM calendar as a standalone system without connecting to Google or Apple Calendar. Simply don't click the sync buttons.

Can I sync with Outlook?

Not directly, but you can sync Outlook with Google Calendar, then connect Google Calendar to the CRM for indirect Outlook integration.

Does sync work internationally?

Yes, calendar sync works globally and handles different time zones automatically. Events display in your local time zone wherever you are.
