Reading Trend Signals

Raw numbers tell you where you are, but trends tell you where you're going. Here's how to spot momentum shifts:

üî• Heating Up Signals

  • DOM dropping 20%+ month-over-month
  • New listings selling within first week
  • $/SF rising 2%+ in 90 days
  • Months of supply under 2.5
  • Multiple offer stories increasing
  • Price reduction rate under 8%

‚ùÑÔ∏è Cooling Down Signals

  • DOM increasing 20%+ month-over-month
  • Active inventory growing weekly
  • $/SF flat or declining 90 days
  • Months of supply over 4.0
  • Price reductions over 15% of listings
  • New listings sitting without showings
