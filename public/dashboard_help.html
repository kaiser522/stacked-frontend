What is the Dashboard?

The Dashboard is your main control center that provides a comprehensive overview of your real estate business performance. It displays key metrics, trends, recent activities, and quick action buttons to help you stay on top of your business operations.

When you log into your CRM, the Dashboard is the first thing you'll see, giving you an immediate snapshot of how your business is performing today.

Understanding Your Metrics

  • Total Clients: The total number of active clients in your database, including both buyers and sellers
  • Active Listings: Properties currently listed for sale that haven't closed or been withdrawn
  • Revenue This Month: Total commission and revenue generated in the current month
  • Scheduled Showings: Number of property showings you have scheduled for upcoming dates
ðŸ’¡ Pro Tip: Each metric shows a percentage change from the previous month (green for increases, red for decreases) to help you quickly identify trends in your business performance.

Monthly Performance Chart

The Monthly Performance chart displays your business activity over the past 6 months, showing two key data points:

  • Blue Bars: Represent the number of new listings added each month
  • Green Bars: Represent the number of sales closed each month

How to Read the Chart

  • The vertical axis shows the quantity/value scale
  • The horizontal axis shows months from January to June
  • Higher bars indicate better performance for that month
  • Compare month-to-month to identify seasonal trends and growth patterns

Revenue Trend Analysis

The Revenue Trend line chart shows your monthly revenue progression over time, helping you:

  • Track revenue growth or decline patterns
  • Identify seasonal fluctuations in your business
  • Set realistic goals based on historical performance
  • Hover over data points to see exact revenue figures for each month
ðŸ“ˆ Analysis Tip: A consistently upward trend indicates healthy business growth. Sudden drops may indicate market changes or the need to adjust your sales strategy.

Recent Activity Feed

The Recent Activity section keeps you updated on the latest happenings in your business. Activity items are color-coded for quick identification:

Activity Types

  • "new" (Blue): New client inquiries and fresh leads
  • "completed" (Green): Successfully completed tasks, deals, or milestones
  • "signed" (Purple): Contracts and agreements that have been signed
  • "scheduled" (Orange): Upcoming appointments and scheduled activities
  • "active" (Gray): New listings that are now active on the market

Understanding Activity Timestamps

Each activity shows when it occurred (e.g., "2 hours ago", "1 day ago") so you can prioritize your follow-up actions accordingly.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Metrics Not Updating

  • Refresh your browser page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Check if you've recently added new data that should reflect in the metrics

Charts Not Loading

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Try viewing the dashboard in a different browser
  • Disable browser extensions that might block content

Recent Activity Not Showing

  • Verify that activities were properly saved in their respective sections
  • Check if your activity filters are set correctly
  • Contact support if activities are missing after confirming they were saved
