The Four Market Cycle Phases

Understanding where your market is in the cycle is essential for strategic planning:

üöÄ

Expansion Phase

Characteristics:

  • Rising prices and sales volume
  • Low inventory levels
  • Multiple offers common
  • Quick sales (under 30 days)
  • High buyer competition

Duration: 2-5 years typically

Your Focus: Volume and efficiency

Peak Phase

Characteristics:

  • Maximum prices reached
  • Sales pace starts slowing
  • Inventory begins increasing
  • First signs of buyer hesitation
  • Media attention on "hot market"

Duration: 6-18 months

Your Focus: Positioning and preparation

üìâ

Contraction Phase

Characteristics:

  • Declining prices and volume
  • Rising inventory levels
  • Longer days on market
  • Price reductions common
  • Buyer negotiation power

Duration: 1-3 years

Your Focus: Value and expertise

üèîÔ∏è

Trough Phase

Characteristics:

  • Prices stabilize at low point
  • Volume remains low
  • High inventory but slowing growth
  • Only motivated buyers active
  • Media reports "market bottom"

Duration: 6 months - 2 years

Your Focus: Investment and preparation
