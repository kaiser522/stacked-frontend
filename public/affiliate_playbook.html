Your Path to Affiliate Success

This playbook walks you through everything you need to know to succeed as a Stacked Technologies affiliate. From getting your first referral link to tracking your commissions and scaling your income.

Getting Started

1

Access Your Affiliate Dashboard

Once approved for the affiliate program, you'll receive login credentials for your affiliate dashboard.

What You'll Find in Your Dashboard:

Commission tracking, referral links, marketing materials, performance analytics, and payout history.

Setup Checklist:

Log into your affiliate dashboard
Complete your profile information
Set up payment method for commissions
Review affiliate terms and commission structure
2

Generate Your Referral Links

Your unique referral links are the foundation of your affiliate success. Each link tracks back to you for commission credit.

Link Format:

https://get-stacked.com/partners/<handle>?ref=<affiliate_id>&utm_source=<handle>&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=<campaign>

Link Customization Options:

Create different campaign links to track which promotion methods work best (social media, email, blog posts, etc.).

Link Setup Tasks:

Generate your main referral link
Create campaign-specific links for different channels
Test all links to ensure they work properly
Save links in a secure document for easy access
3

Download Marketing Materials

Access professionally designed marketing materials to support your promotional efforts.

Available Resources:

  • Video script templates for TikTok/Instagram
  • Email swipe files and sequences
  • Social media post templates
  • Competitor comparison charts
  • FAQ cheat sheets
  • Display ad templates

Important Usage Rules:

Always use your unique referral links in all marketing materials. Do not post links on coupon or deal sites as this violates program terms.

Promotion Strategies

1

Social Media Marketing

Leverage social platforms to reach real estate professionals where they're already spending time.

Best Platforms for Real Estate:

  • TikTok: Short videos showing system benefits and success stories
  • Instagram: Reels, Stories, and posts about productivity and tools
  • LinkedIn: Professional content targeting real estate agents
  • Facebook: Groups, pages, and targeted real estate communities
  • YouTube: Longer-form tutorials and system demonstrations

Content Ideas That Convert:

Behind-the-scenes of your workflow, before/after productivity comparisons, client success stories, tool tutorials, and industry tips.

Social Media Action Plan:

Choose 2-3 primary platforms to focus on
Create content calendar for consistent posting
Record first video using provided script templates
Add referral link to bio/profile
2

Email Marketing

Build an email list of real estate professionals and nurture them with valuable content.

Email Strategy Framework:

  • Create lead magnets (free guides, checklists, templates)
  • Use provided email sequences to nurture prospects
  • Share industry insights and productivity tips
  • Include soft pitches for Stacked Technologies
  • Track open rates and click-through rates

High-Converting Lead Magnets:

"5 CRM Hacks That Double Follow-Up Success," "Real Estate Productivity Checklist," "Lead Management Templates," "Compliance Quick Reference Guide."

Email Compliance:

Always follow CAN-SPAM requirements: include unsubscribe links, use legitimate from addresses, and don't use misleading subject lines.

SEO-Friendly Article Ideas:

Build relationships within the real estate community to create organic referral opportunities.

Networking Strategies:

Commission Tracking

1

Understanding the Commission Structure

Familiarize yourself with how and when you earn commissions to set proper expectations.

Period Commission Rate Details
Month 1 30% Of customer's first monthly payment
Months 2-12 10% Recurring monthly commissions
After Month 12 0% 12-month cap per customer

Activity Requirement:

Refer 2+ new paying customers each calendar month to maintain recurring commission eligibility.

Important Notes:

Commissions are held for 14 days after each charge. Minimum $150 in approved commissions required for monthly payout. New customers only - no self-referrals.

2

Monitor Your Dashboard

Regular dashboard monitoring helps you track performance and optimize your strategy.

Key Metrics to Track:

  • Clicks: How many people clicked your referral links
  • Conversions: How many became paying customers
  • Conversion Rate: Percentage of clicks that convert
  • Pending Commissions: Earnings in 14-day hold period
  • Approved Commissions: Ready for payout
  • Monthly New Customers: Progress toward 2+ requirement

Optimization Tips:

Track which traffic sources convert best, test different messaging, and focus on channels with highest conversion rates.

Weekly Tracking Routine:

Check total clicks and traffic sources
Review new conversions and customer details
Calculate conversion rates by traffic source
Monitor progress toward monthly 2+ customer requirement
Check pending vs approved commission status
3

Attribution & Tracking Rules

Understanding how attribution works ensures you get proper credit for your referrals.

Attribution Details:

  • 30-day last-click: Customer must purchase within 30 days of clicking your link
  • New customers only: System blocks by email, phone, and payment method
  • Last-click wins: If customer clicks multiple affiliate links, last one gets credit
  • Referral links only: No public coupon codes or deal site posting allowed

What Doesn't Count:

Self-referrals, existing customers, refunded/chargebacked payments, or customers acquired through prohibited methods (spam, brand-bidding, coupon sites).

Best Practices for Attribution:

Use campaign tracking in your links, focus on warm leads who are ready to buy, and follow up within the 30-day window to maintain your attribution.

4

Payout Process

Know when and how you'll receive your commission payments.

Payout Schedule:

  • Hold Period: 14 days after customer payment clears
  • Payment Date: By the 15th of each month for approved commissions
  • Minimum Threshold: $150 in approved commissions required
  • Sub-minimum: Amounts below $150 do not roll over to next month

Maximizing Payouts:

Focus on consistent monthly performance to hit the $150 minimum and maintain the 2+ new customer requirement for recurring commissions.

Clawback Policy:

Refunds, chargebacks, fraud, or payment reversals will be deducted from future commissions.

Scaling & Growth

1

Optimize Your Top Channels

Double down on what's working and eliminate what isn't to maximize your ROI.

Performance Analysis:

  • Identify your highest-converting traffic sources
  • Calculate cost per acquisition for paid channels
  • Measure time investment vs. commission return
  • Test different messaging and content formats
  • Scale successful campaigns gradually

80/20 Rule for Affiliates:

Typically 20% of your efforts will generate 80% of your results. Find those high-impact activities and focus there.

Monthly Optimization Tasks:

Analyze conversion rates by traffic source
Pause or eliminate low-performing channels
Increase effort/budget on top performers
Test new content formats or platforms
2

Build Systems & Automation

Create repeatable systems to scale your affiliate business without proportionally increasing your time investment.

Automation Opportunities:

  • Email autoresponder sequences for lead nurturing
  • Social media scheduling tools for consistent posting
  • Template responses for common questions
  • Affiliate link management and tracking systems
  • Content creation workflows and batching

Recommended Tools:

Email: ConvertKit/Mailchimp, Social: Buffer/Hootsuite, Analytics: Google Analytics, Link Management: Bitly/Pretty Links, Content: Canva/Loom.

3

Expand Your Reach

Once you've optimized your core channels, strategically expand to new audiences and platforms.

Expansion Strategies:

  • Partner with real estate influencers or coaches
  • Guest post on industry blogs and publications
  • Speak at real estate conferences and events
  • Create affiliate partnerships with complementary businesses
  • Develop premium content or courses as lead magnets

Scaling Safely:

Don't expand too quickly. Master 1-2 channels first, then gradually add new ones while maintaining quality and compliance.

4

Long-term Success Strategies

Build a sustainable affiliate business that grows over time.

Success Principles:

  • Consistency: Regular promotion beats sporadic bursts
  • Value-first: Always lead with helpful content
  • Relationship building: Focus on long-term connections
  • Continuous learning: Stay updated on industry trends
  • Data-driven decisions: Let metrics guide your strategy

Monthly Goal Framework:

Set specific targets for new customers, commission earnings, content creation, and audience growth. Review and adjust monthly based on performance.
