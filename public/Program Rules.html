Affiliate Program — Balanced Rules (Final)

Commission Structure

  • Month 1: 30% commission
  • Months 2–12: 10% commission
  • Cap: 12 paid months per referred customer (no commissions after month 12)

Activity Target

Requirement: Refer 2+ new paid customers each calendar month.

1st Miss (< 2 in a month):
  • No penalty
  • Send automated heads‑up to the affiliate
2nd Consecutive Miss:
  • Recurring commissions pause for the next month
  • No back‑pay for the paused month
  • Once the affiliate hits ≥ 2 in any later month, recurring resumes the following month
3rd Consecutive Miss:
  • Recurring commissions end permanently starting the next month
  • Account is subject to termination
  • Month‑1 bounties already cleared still pay
  • Affiliate may reapply later; acceptance not guaranteed; prior streams do not resume

Payouts

  • Hold: Net‑14 — eligibility begins 14 days after each charge
  • Schedule: Paid by the 15th of each month for approved amounts
  • Minimum: $150 in approved commissions required to receive a payout for that month (sub‑minimum amounts roll forward)

Attribution & Anti‑Abuse

  • Attribution: 30‑day last‑click
  • Referral links only (hide public coupon field)
  • New customers only (block by email, phone, and card fingerprint)
  • No self‑referrals, spam, coupon‑site posting, or brand‑bidding
  • Clawbacks on refunds, chargebacks, fraud, or payment reversal

Example Timeline (for clarity)

  • Jan: 1 new (Miss #1) → No penalty; send heads‑up.
  • Feb: 1 new (Miss #2) → March recurring is paused (no back‑pay).
  • Mar: 0 new (Miss #3) → April onward, recurring ends permanently; possible termination.

“New paid” = a brand‑new customer whose first charge clears the 14‑day hold and is not refunded/charged back.
