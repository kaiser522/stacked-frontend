Phone Script: Testimonial Collection

Call Timing: 1-2 weeks after closing, when the excitement is still fresh but the stress has settled.

Opening:

"Hi [Client Name]! This is [Your Name]. I hope you're settling into your new home well! I'm calling because I wanted to thank you again for trusting me with your [sale/purchase], and I have a quick favor to ask if you have just a minute."

The Ask:

"As you know, most of my business comes from referrals from wonderful clients like you. Would you be comfortable sharing a quick testimonial about your experience? It really helps other families understand what it's like to work with me."

If they agree:

"That's wonderful, thank you! I can either send you a quick online form to fill out, or if you prefer, I can ask you a few questions right now and write it up for you to approve. What would work better for you?"

If they prefer phone interview:

Key Questions to Ask:

  1. "What was the best part of working with me?"
  2. "How did I help make the process easier for you?"
  3. "What would you tell other families considering working with me?"
  4. "Is there anything specific you'd want people to know about my service?"
  5. "Would you recommend me to your friends and family?"

Closing:

"Thank you so much for taking the time to share that. I'll write this up and send it to you for approval before I use it anywhere. And remember, I'm always here if you or anyone you know has real estate questions!"
