Estate Sale Overview

Estate sales require special handling due to legal requirements, multiple decision-makers, and court oversight. This checklist ensures compliance and smooth transactions.

⚠️ Legal Notice: Always recommend sellers consult with probate attorney. Requirements vary by state and estate complexity.

Key Estate Sale Characteristics

Court Oversight: Many decisions require court approval
Multiple Parties: Several heirs may need to agree
Property Condition: Often needs repairs or cleanout
Timeline: Legal process can extend closing dates
