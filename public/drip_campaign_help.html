What is the Drip Campaign Builder?

The Drip Campaign Builder is a powerful automation tool that allows you to create and manage email sequences that automatically nurture your leads over time. Instead of manually sending individual follow-up emails, you can set up pre-written sequences that deliver the right message at the right time based on your client's journey.

Drip campaigns help you stay top-of-mind with prospects, provide valuable information, and guide leads through your sales funnel‚Äîall while saving you hours of manual work each week. The system comes with pre-built templates for common real estate scenarios, or you can create custom campaigns tailored to your specific needs.

Creating a New Campaign

Click the "+ Create Campaign" button to build a custom drip campaign from scratch:

  1. Click "+ Create Campaign" in the top-left corner
  2. Enter a campaign name and description
  3. Choose a campaign icon and color for easy identification
  4. Click "Add Email" to create your first email in the sequence
  5. Write your email subject line and body content
  6. Set the delay (how many days after enrollment or previous email)
  7. Add more emails to build your complete sequence
  8. Preview the campaign to review all emails
  9. Save and start enrolling clients

Email Timing Options

  • Days After Enrollment: Send X days after client joins campaign
  • Days After Previous Email: Send X days after they received last email
  • Specific Date: Send on a particular date (for seasonal campaigns)
  • Day of Week: Always send on specific days (e.g., every Monday)
Campaign Best Practice: Space emails 2-4 days apart for active campaigns, or 5-7 days for longer nurture sequences. Too frequent feels spammy; too spread out loses momentum.

Enrolling Clients in Campaigns

Single Client Enrollment

  1. Click "Enroll Client" on the desired campaign card
  2. Select a client from your contact list
  3. Choose start date (immediate or scheduled)
  4. Confirm enrollment
  5. Client begins receiving emails according to sequence

Bulk Enrollment

  • Select multiple clients from your list
  • Choose "Bulk Actions" and select "Enroll in Campaign"
  • Pick the campaign to enroll them in
  • All selected clients start the sequence
  • Useful for adding entire lead lists to nurture campaigns

Automatic Enrollment Triggers

Set up rules to automatically enroll clients based on actions:

  • New Lead: Automatically add to New Lead Nurture when created
  • Stage Change: Move to different campaign when deal stage changes
  • Tag Applied: Enroll when specific tag is added to contact
  • Form Submission: Auto-enroll from website form captures
  • Event Attendance: Add to follow-up after open house attendance

Campaign Best Practices

Writing Effective Emails

  • Compelling Subject Lines: Clear, benefit-focused, 6-8 words
  • Personalized Content: Use merge tags and reference specific situations
  • Clear Value: Every email should provide useful information or insights
  • Single Call-to-Action: One clear next step per email
  • Mobile-Friendly: Short paragraphs, scannable format
  • Professional Tone: Friendly but authoritative

Campaign Strategy

  • Match Campaign to Buyer Stage: First-time buyers need education; ready buyers need properties
  • Progressive Value: Each email should build on previous ones
  • Strategic Timing: Don't rush the sequence‚Äînurturing takes time
  • Clear Exit: End with strong call-to-action or transition
  • Monitor and Adjust: Review performance monthly and refine

When to Use Each Template

Managing Active Campaigns

Pausing a Campaign

  • Click Edit on the campaign card
  • Toggle the "Active" switch to off
  • No new emails send, but enrolled clients remain in sequence
  • Reactivate anytime to resume sending

Removing Clients from Campaigns

  • Go to View Enrolled for the campaign
  • Find the client in the list
  • Click "Remove" or "Unenroll"
  • Client stops receiving emails immediately
  • Can re-enroll them later if needed

Duplicating Campaigns

  • Use "Duplicate" option in campaign menu
  • Creates copy of entire sequence
  • Modify for different audience or test variations
  • Useful for creating seasonal versions

Deleting Campaigns

  • Only delete campaigns with zero enrolled clients
  • Remove all enrollments first
  • Click Delete and confirm
  • Campaign and all emails permanently removed

Compliance and Legal Considerations

CAN-SPAM Compliance

All drip campaigns must comply with CAN-SPAM Act requirements:

  • Accurate "From" name and email address
  • Honest subject lines (no deceptive headers)
  • Physical mailing address in footer
  • Clear unsubscribe mechanism
  • Honor opt-out requests within 10 business days

Real Estate Email Best Practices

  • Only email people who have given permission
  • Include license number in email signature
  • Follow fair housing guidelines in content
  • Don't make misleading claims about properties
  • Respect state-specific real estate advertising laws

Troubleshooting

Emails Not Sending

  • Check campaign is set to "Active"
  • Verify client email addresses are valid
  • Ensure sending schedule is configured correctly
  • Check if you've reached daily sending limits
  • Confirm email account is connected and authenticated

Low Open Rates

  • Test different subject lines
  • Check if emails are landing in spam
  • Verify sending from recognized domain
  • Send at optimal times (Tuesday-Thursday, 9 AM-2 PM)
  • Clean your list of inactive addresses

High Unsubscribe Rates

  • Review email frequency (may be too often)
  • Ensure content is valuable and relevant
  • Check that you're targeting right audience
  • Verify contacts opted in to receive emails
  • Improve content quality and personalization

Merge Tags Not Working

  • Check syntax: should be {{field_name}}
  • Ensure client has data for that field
  • Verify field names match exactly
  • Preview with test client to check rendering
  • Contact support if issues persist
