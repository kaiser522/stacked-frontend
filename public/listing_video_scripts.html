Property Tour Video Script

Property: 123 Oak Street, Beverly Hills, CA

Duration: 2-3 minutes | Style: Professional Tour

Opening Hook (0:00 - 0:15)

[Wide exterior shot of property]
Welcome to this stunning 3-bedroom, 2.5-bathroom home located in the desirable Beverly Hills neighborhood. I'm Sarah Johnson with Premium Realty, and I can't wait to show you everything this beautiful property has to offer.
Timing: 15 seconds | Keep energy high and engaging

Exterior Features (0:15 - 0:45)

[Walk around exterior, showing key features]
Let's start with the impressive curb appeal. This home features beautiful landscaping and a welcoming front porch. The two-car garage provides excellent storage, and you'll love the spacious backyard perfect for entertaining.
Timing: 30 seconds | Show each feature as you mention it

Main Living Areas (0:45 - 1:30)

[Enter home, tour living room, dining room, kitchen]
Step inside and you're greeted by an open floor plan that's perfect for both daily living and entertaining. The living room features beautiful hardwood floors and large windows that flood the space with natural light. The heart of this home is definitely the kitchen, which boasts granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, and plenty of cabinet space. This is where memories are made!
Timing: 45 seconds | Move smoothly between rooms

Bedrooms & Bathrooms (1:30 - 2:15)

[Tour bedrooms and bathrooms]
Upstairs, you'll find the spacious master suite with walk-in closet and ensuite bathroom. The additional bedrooms are perfect for family, guests, or a home office. All bathrooms have been recently updated with modern fixtures and finishes.
Timing: 45 seconds | Highlight storage and natural light

Closing & Call to Action (2:15 - 2:30)

[Return to living room or exterior for closing]
This beautiful home at 123 Oak Street is priced at $485,000 and won't last long in today's market. If you'd like to schedule a private showing or have any questions, give me a call at (555) 123-4567 or visit my website. Don't let this amazing opportunity pass you by!
Timing: 15 seconds | Include clear contact information

Production Notes

Equipment Needed: DSLR camera or smartphone with stabilizer, external microphone, good lighting

Best Times to Film: Golden hour (1 hour before sunset) for exterior shots, well-lit daytime for interior

Key Tips:
  • Keep camera movements smooth and steady
  • Film each room from multiple angles
  • Capture unique features and selling points
  • Ensure good audio quality - consider voice-over in post
  • Include neighborhood shots and local amenities
