The Weekly Content Calendar

Consistent posting schedule that maximizes engagement:

Your 7-Day Content Framework

Monday: Market Monday (stats and trends)

Tuesday: Tip Tuesday (educational content)

Wednesday: Behind-the-scenes or team spotlight

Thursday: Throwback/success story

Friday: Feature Friday (listing or neighborhood)

Saturday: Community events or local love

Sunday: Motivational or personal reflection

Pro Tip: Post at consistent times when your audience is most active. Test different times and track engagement to find your sweet spot.

Engagement Tactics That Build Relationships

It's not enough to post‚Äîyou need to create conversations:

The 4-Hour Rule

Respond to all comments and messages within 4 hours during business days. This shows you're responsive and builds trust with potential clients.

Question-Based Posts

End posts with questions to drive engagement:

  • "What's your biggest concern about buying in today's market?"
  • "What home feature is absolutely non-negotiable for you?"
  • "If you could change one thing about your current neighborhood, what would it be?"

Value-Add Comments

When engaging on others' posts, provide genuine value, not sales pitches. Share insights, ask thoughtful questions, or offer helpful resources.

Converting Social Media Followers to Leads

Strategic calls-to-action that feel natural, not salesy:

Soft CTAs (Use 80% of time)

  • "DM me for more details"
  • "Comment if you'd like the full report"
  • "Tag a friend who needs to see this"
  • "Save this post for later"
  • "What questions do you have?"

Direct CTAs (Use 20% of time)

  • "Ready to start looking? Let's chat"
  • "Thinking of selling? Get a free estimate"
  • "Schedule your buyer consultation"
  • "Get pre-approved in 24 hours"
